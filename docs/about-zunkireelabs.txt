1. Company Overview

Company Name: ZunkireeLabs
Founded: 2026
Headquarters: Kathmandu, Nepal
Type: AI-first software and technology company

ZunkireeLabs is a product-focused AI and software engineering company that builds intelligent digital systems and scalable business software.

The company combines AI engineering, data systems, and modern software architecture to help organizations transform how their systems, data, and customer experiences operate.

While headquartered in Nepal, ZunkireeLabs is designed to operate with a global mindset, building products and solutions that can serve businesses internationally.

The company follows a product-first strategy, where services support revenue and market access, while long-term growth comes from scalable software platforms.

2. Mission

To build intelligent software platforms that connect data, systems, and human interaction through AI, enabling businesses to operate more efficiently and deliver better digital experiences.

3. Vision

ZunkireeLabs aims to become a global AI software company originating from Nepal, building products that power the next generation of intelligent digital experiences.

The long-term vision is to develop a portfolio of AI-native software platforms that serve multiple industries worldwide.

4. Core Philosophy

ZunkireeLabs operates with several key principles:

Product-first mindset

6. Products

ZunkireeLabs is building several internal products designed for long-term scalability.

Zunkiree Search (Flagship Product)

Zunkiree Search is an AI-native search and interaction platform that allows businesses to connect their data and knowledge to natural language queries.

Instead of traditional search, users can ask questions directly, and the system provides accurate, contextual answers based on the company’s data.

The platform works by combining:

Retrieval systems

AI language models

knowledge indexing

intelligent context generation

Key Benefits

Faster information discovery

Reduced customer friction

Improved user experience

AI-powered knowledge access

The system is designed as a reusable infrastructure layer that can be embedded into websites and platforms.

Gaamma (Manufacturing ERP)

Gaamma is an enterprise resource planning platform designed for manufacturing businesses.

It focuses on improving operational visibility, process efficiency, and production management.

The platform demonstrates ZunkireeLabs’ capability to build large-scale enterprise software systems.

Dental AI Assistant (Upcoming)

A specialized AI assistant designed to support dental clinics with:

patient interactions

operational assistance

workflow support

This product represents ZunkireeLabs’ strategy to develop vertical AI solutions for specific industries.
